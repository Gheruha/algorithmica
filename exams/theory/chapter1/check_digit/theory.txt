The check digit of a number is obtained by adding its digits, then adding the digits of this sum, until the sum obtained is a single-digit number. This last digit is called the check digit.

Example:
n = 4568258
cd(4568258) = 38
cd(38) = 11
cd(11) = 2

2 is the check number
